# Prior art

blogoboros invents nothing. Every post in the ring is a pastiche of a real,
thriving genre: the developer blog whose first — and frequently only — post is
about the blog itself. Below, a bibliography of specimens collected in June
2026. All links were live search results at collection time; like all blogs,
they may since have migrated.

## Tropes of the genre

Reproduced faithfully in each blogoboros post:

1. The title is "Hello, world!", "How I built this blog", or "Goodbye X, Hello Y"
2. An opening confession or apology for the silence the rewrite itself caused
3. The full stack inventory: generator, hosting, Markdown, syntax highlighting, dark mode, RSS
4. The justification trilogy: own your content; static is fast; "writing will make me a better engineer"
5. Era-correct grievances against the previous platform
6. A build-time benchmark nobody asked for
7. A closing promise of regular future posts, falsified by the archive
8. Self-awareness acknowledged mid-post, then performed anyway
9. Rewrites numbered like versions ("a fifth time")
